Linus Wolfe Tolliday

bio

I am a director based in Melbourne with an
interest in questioning how stories are told and
using film as a search for meaning.

I am autistic and attribute to this a fascination
with social interactions as having game-like
qualities and rules.

I studied at the Victorian College of the Arts;
I directed Journey's End (2023) as my graduating
film, which was shot on 16mm film. I have since
worked in archive producing and research in
episodic and feature work.

I am currently writing my next short, titled
Quarters, about a misdelivered letter and the
accidental recipient's attempt to return it to
the intended recipient.
